What is Fantasy Ethiopia?

Fantasy Ethiopia is a fantasy football game, where a user can create a team of his from set of players available on the application

How it works?

Enjoy this remarkably simple game with your friends and see who wins! Each player in a team will be awarded points by the admin, which is based on the performance of the week. Each user's team will have a cumulative gamescore for a gameweek based on the players he chose to create his team with.
